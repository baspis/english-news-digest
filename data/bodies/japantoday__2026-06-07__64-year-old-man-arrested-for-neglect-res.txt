A 64-year-old man has been arrested in Ichikikushikino City, Kagoshima Prefecture, on suspicion of neglecting his 92-year-old mother, who was living with him at the time, leading to her death.
Police said Masayuki Maehata is accused of leaving his mother, Tsugie, by herself between early April and early May, despite her need for care, Kagoshima TV reported.
The incident came to light on May 8 when a relative who had come to check on Tsugie's well-being found her dead in her bed.
Maehata subsequently disappeared, but on June 1, he visited a police box in Tokushima Prefecture and stated that he was "tired of caring for his mother." He was taken to Ichikikushikino City for questioning and arrested on Thursday.
