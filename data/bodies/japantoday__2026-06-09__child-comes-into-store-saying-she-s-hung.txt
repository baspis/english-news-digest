Police in Kushiro, Hokkaido, have arrested a 25-year-old woman, a self-proclaimed tattoo artist, and her 27-year-old boyfriend, a company employee, on suspicion of child abandonment. The two are suspected of leaving the woman’s preschool-aged daughter alone at the man's house for about 11 1/2 hours from 10 p.m. on Saturday while they were out drinking, Hokkaido Broadcasting Corp reported. The girl showed up at a convenience store at around 9:30 a.m. on Sunday. An employee called police and reported that a girl had come in alone, saying she was hungry. At about the same time, the girl’s mother called police and said her daughter was missing. The girl was unharmed and in good health. Police said the mother and her boyfriend have admitted to leaving their daughter alone while they went out drinking together at several establishments.
