Grammar focus

Inverted attribution (..., X said)

In news writing, the source is often placed at the end: main clause + comma + subject + reporting verb.

Japan posted a current account surplus ..., the Finance Ministry said Monday.

報道文では主文の後ろに「〜が述べた」を置く倒置が多い。話者の主張と情報源を分けて、客観的に見せる文体。

thanks to + noun (cause)

Use 'thanks to' before a noun phrase to show the reason for a positive result.

...in April, thanks to returns from robust overseas investments and rises in exports.

良い結果の原因を示すとき 'thanks to + 名詞' を使う。because of より成果への寄与が強調される。

Percent change + from ... to ...

Economic news often uses: verb + X% + from a year earlier + to [amount].

Primary income rose 15.3 percent from a year earlier to 4.21 trillion yen.

経済ニュースでは「前年比◯%増し、◯円に達した」を一つの文型で表す。from a year earlier と to の組み合わせに注意。

Participial clause (V-ing / past participle)

Use -ing or past participle phrases to add background or cause without a new clause.

the Middle East conflict, stemming from the launch of U.S.-Israeli attacks on Iran

分詞構文で補足情報を挿入できる。関係代名詞節より簡潔で、ニュースでは背景説明に頻出。
