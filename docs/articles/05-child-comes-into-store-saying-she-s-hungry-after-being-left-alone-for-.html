Grammar focus

Passive + participle (being left / saying)

Use being + past participle after prepositions (after, by) or as a reduced clause with -ing to add background detail.

after being left alone for 11 hours ... saying she was hungry

「放置された後」「お腹が空いていると言いながら」のように、受動や付帯状況を簡潔に足す報道英語の型。

on suspicion of + noun

Formal news phrase meaning someone was arrested or investigated because they are believed to have committed an offense.

「〜の疑いで」という公式・報道向けの前置き表現。動詞は arrest, detain, question などとよく結びつく。

Reported speech with past perfect

In news reports, use that-clauses; shift tenses back when the reporting verb is past (said/reported).
