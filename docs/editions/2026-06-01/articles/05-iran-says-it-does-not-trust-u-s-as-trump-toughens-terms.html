Grammar focus

Reporting verbs with that-clauses

Use verbs like warn, report, say, insist + (that) + clause to introduce statements in news writing.

Ghalibaf warned the United States was not to be trusted.

ニュースでは warn/report/say/insist などに that節（that省略可）を続けて発言や報道内容を伝える。語順は「人物→動詞→内容」が基本。

Concessive while / though / however

while/though introduce contrast; however marks contrast between sentences.

While Tehran has insisted its program is civilian, allies suspect otherwise.

while/though は「〜だが」という譲歩・対比。however は文頭で前の内容と対比する。外交記事で立場の違いを示すのに頻出。

With + noun + -ing participle clause

with + NP + V-ing adds accompanying detail or secondary action.

exchanges are ongoing, with both parties regularly proposing amendments

with + 名詞 + 現在分詞で「〜しながら」「〜という状況で」という付帯情報を追加する。ニュースで背景状況を簡潔に足すときに便利。

Defining relative clauses (who/which/that)

Use who/which/that to add essential background about people, places, and events.

a deal that would lift blockades that have strangled a vital route

関係代名詞節で人物・場所・出来事の必要な背景を足す。記事では連鎖する関係節で複雑な状況を一文にまとめることが多い。
