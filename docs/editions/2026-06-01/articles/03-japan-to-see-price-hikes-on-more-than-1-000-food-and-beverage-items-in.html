Japan to see price hikes on more than 1,000 food and beverage items in June

Japan Today Business · 2026-06-01

帝国データバンクの調査によると、日本では6月に1,078品目の食品・飲料が値上げされ、5月の84品目から大幅に増加する。中東情勢に伴うコスト上昇を消費者に転嫁する動きが背景にあり、年間では1万品目超の見込み。調味料や加工食品が特に多い。

Grammar focus

News source attribution (says / according to)

Place the source at the end (X says) or beginning (According to X) to show where information comes from.

..., a research institute says. / According to a report by the institute, ...

英語のニュースでは情報源を文末や文頭で明示する。a research institute says や according to a report の形が典型。

Passive voice for events and impact (be affected by / be used to)

Use passive structures to focus on what happens to products or people rather than who caused it.

22.7 percent of the items were affected by the Iran war.

影響や用途を述べるとき受動態を使い、原因や主体より結果に焦点を当てる。

Participle clauses (stemming from / including / facing)

Use -ing or past participle phrases to add cause, examples, or description without a new clause.

costs stemming from the Middle East crisis / items facing price hikes

現在分詞や過去分詞の句で原因・例・説明を簡潔に付け加える。関係代名詞節の短縮形として機能する。

Do so substitution

Use do so to avoid repeating a verb phrase mentioned earlier.

If the figure exceeds 10,000, it will be the fifth consecutive year to do so.

先に出た動詞句を繰り返さず do so で代用する。フォーマルな文章で重要。
