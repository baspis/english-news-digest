AI giant Anthropic confidentially files for IPO

Japan Today Tech · 2026-06-02
AI大手アンソロピックが、Claudeチャットボットの開発元として月曜日に機密提出でIPO（新規株式公開）手続きを行ったと発表した。同社は直前に650億ドルの資金調達で企業価値9650億ドルに達しており、OpenAIを上回る評価額で上場を目指す。一方、計算資源不足や国防総省との法的争いなど、成長に伴う課題も抱えている。

Grammar focus

Participial phrase at sentence start

A past participle or -ing phrase can open a sentence to add background (Founded in..., Asked by..., Located in...).

Founded in 2021 by siblings Dario and Daniela Amodei, Anthropic has positioned itself as a safety-focused alternative.

文頭の過去分詞や-ing句で背景情報を先に示す手法。日本語の「〜によって設立された〜は」のように訳すと自然。

Reporting verbs in news (said, added, announced)

Use said/added/announced + that-clause (or direct quote) to attribute statements; tense may shift in indirect speech.

"The number of shares to be offered and the price have not yet been set," the company added.

ニュースでは発言を伝える動詞が定型。直接引用なら時制はそのまま、間接話法なら文脈で時制がずれることがある。

Non-defining relative clauses with commas

Use which/who clauses set off by commas to add extra information about a noun; no 'that' for non-defining clauses.

Anthropic's valuation puts it ahead of OpenAI, which was valued at $852 billion in March.

カンマで挟む関係代名詞節は補足説明。日本語では別の文や「〜で、こちらは…」と分けて訳すと読みやすい。

With + noun + past participle

'With + object + past participle' describes an accompanying circumstance or expectation.

Anthropic's valuation puts it ahead of OpenAI, with a filing expected imminently.

with + 名詞 + 過去分詞で「〜という状況で／〜と見られて」という付帯状況を簡潔に表す。
