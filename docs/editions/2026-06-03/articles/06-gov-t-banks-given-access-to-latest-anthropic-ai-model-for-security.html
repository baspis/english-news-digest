Gov't, banks given access to latest Anthropic AI model for security

Japan Today Tech · 2026-06-03
日本政府と一部の銀行が、高度なサイバーセキュリティ機能を備えた米スタートアップAnthropicの新AIモデル「Claude Mythos」へのアクセスを認められたと、政府が水曜日に明らかにした。高度なAI主導のサイバー攻撃への懸念が高まる中、財務大臣片山さつき氏やサイバーセキュリティ担当大臣松本尚氏が、日本の金融機関や国全体の防衛力強化に資すると歓迎した。

Japan's government and some banks have been given access to U.S. startup Anthropic's new Claude Mythos artificial intelligence model with advanced cybersecurity capabilities, the government said Wednesday, as concerns mount about cyberattacks led by sophisticated AI.

Grammar focus

Passive: have been given access to

Use 'be given access to + noun' to report that permission to use something has been granted.

Major Japanese banks have been given access to the new AI model.

「～へのアクセスを与えられた」は報道でよく使う受動表現。誰が与えたかは文脈や別節で示されることが多い。

Reporting clauses with said / according to

Place the source (said..., according to...) near the reported information, often at the end or after a quote.

The government said Wednesday, as concerns mount...

情報源を文末や引用の後に置くのが英語ニュースの典型。日本語より主語・述語の順が入れ替わることがある。

Participial clauses (vowing / referring / led by)

Use -ing or past participle phrases to add background, reason, or manner without starting a new sentence.

Matsumoto hailed the move, vowing to take steps to strengthen resilience.

分詞構文で背景や付帯行為を簡潔に足す。日本語では「～しながら」「～について」と訳すことが多い。

Formal 'it' patterns: It is ... that / for ... to

Use 'It is + adjective + that/for...' to emphasize evaluation or judgment.

It is unacceptable for institutions to fall behind other countries.

It is unacceptable for A to do B は「AがBすることは許されない」。評価・判断を客観的に述べる硬い表現。
