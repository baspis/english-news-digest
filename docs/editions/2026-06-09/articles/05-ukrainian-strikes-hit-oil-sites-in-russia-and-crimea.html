Grammar focus

Inverted attribution (said X)

News style places the reporting clause (officials said Monday) after the main event, often mid-sentence.

Ukrainian forces struck oil facilities..., Ukrainian and Russian officials said Monday, as part of...

ニュース英語では「〜と当局は述べた」を文中や文末に置き、事実を先に伝える倒置が多い。

Passive voice for casualties

Casualty reports often use passive (were killed/injured) with by-phrase or agent at the end.

Two people were killed and at least 18 injured... by a Russian drone attack

死傷報道では受動態で被害者を主語にし、攻撃主体は by 句や文末に回す。

Present participle for result

Use -ing clauses to show simultaneous result: striking X, injuring Y, sparking Z.

a Ukrainian drone struck a passenger train..., injuring the driver and killing the driver's assistant

現在分詞句で攻撃の直接的結果（負傷・死亡・火災）を簡潔に付加する。

Despite + gerund

Despite + V-ing expresses contrast: X happened even though Y is true.

Despite Putin rejecting Zelensky's public offer of bilateral talks it seems that behind the scenes...

Despite + 動名詞で「〜にもかかわらず」という譲歩の対比を作る。
