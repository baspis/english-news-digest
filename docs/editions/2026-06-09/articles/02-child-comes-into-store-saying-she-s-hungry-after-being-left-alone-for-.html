Grammar focus

on suspicion of + noun

Used in legal/news contexts before charges are proven: arrested/detained on suspicion of [crime].

Police arrested them on suspicion of child abandonment.

有罪が確定する前の段階を示す。「〜の疑いで」という意味で、逮捕・捜査報道の定番表現。

be suspected of + -ing

The preposition of is followed by a gerund (verb + -ing), not an infinitive.

The two are suspected of leaving the child alone.

suspected の後は of + 動名詞。日本語の「〜した疑いがある」に対応する。

Reporting with source attribution (..., X reported/said)

News often places the source (media, police) at the end: [Main clause], [Source] reported.

..., Hokkaido Broadcasting Corp reported.

報道文では出典を文末に置き、事実部分を先に述べる倒置がよく使われる。

Participial phrase: saying / stating ...

A present participle phrase can add speech or manner: ..., saying [clause].

A girl had come in alone, saying she was hungry.

分詞構文 saying ... で「〜と言って」という発言内容を簡潔に追加できる。関係代名詞 who said の省略形。
