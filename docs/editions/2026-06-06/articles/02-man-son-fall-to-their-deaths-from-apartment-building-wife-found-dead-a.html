Man, son fall to their deaths from apartment building; wife found dead at home

Japan Today Crime · 2026-06-06

大阪府富田林市のアパート敷地で、47歳の男性と10歳の息子の遺体が発見された。自宅では妻が刺されて死亡しており、血のついたナイフが近くにあった。警察は殺人・自殺（殺人後自殺）の可能性を含めて調査している。

Grammar focus

Passive voice in crime reporting

Use be + past participle (were found, were taken, were pronounced) to focus on victims and events rather than who performed the action.

The bodies were found on the grounds of an apartment building.

犯罪・事故報道では受動態で「何が起きたか」に焦点を当て、加害者や発見者を明示しない書き方が多い。

identify A as B

identify + object + as + noun/name confirms identity.

Police identified the bodies as Junji Okuda and his son Ibuki.

「AをBと特定する」という身元確認の定型表現。as の後に名前や役割が来る。

After + perfect passive gerund

after having been + past participle shows a completed passive action before the main event.

She was found dead after having been stabbed.

「〜された後に」という完了した受動作を、主な出来事より前に起きたこととして示す。

investigate ... as ...

investigate + object + as + noun frames how authorities classify a case.

They are investigating the deaths as a murder-suicide.

捜査当局が事件をどの類型として扱っているかを示す。as の後に事件の見方・分類が続く。
